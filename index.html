VIDEOS RELATED TO ROAD SAFETY

Road Safety is the measure for people while using the road. The act for lowering road accidents is called road safety. Every year millions of people across the world died due to road accidents. There are various ethics and terms to be followed while using the road. The Common set of rules made up for pedestrians, cyclists, bike-riders, four-wheelers, etc. Pedestrians have to follow the special rules established for the paved surface of the road. Motorists, four-wheeler users and other vehicle users have to follow road signal rule.
